Our Process
How We Work
   1. Consultation: We discuss your business goals, brand, and website needs to make sure we understand exactly what you want.
   2. Planning: We create a site outline and plan the pages/sections needed, then agree on a timeline and milestones.
   3. Design & Development: We build your custom website (HTML/CSS/JavaScript/React) with a focus on modern design and performance.
   4. Testing & Launch: We thoroughly test the site on all devices and browsers, fix any issues, and then launch your site live.
   5. Ongoing Support: After launch, we provide support for updates, improvements, and any additional help you need to keep the website running smoothly.
Each step keeps you involved so the final website perfectly matches your vision. This clear process ensures a smooth project and a website that truly helps your business grow.
